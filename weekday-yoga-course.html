Each class will focus on a different aspect of the asanas being taught in the course. Our Yoga guru will be going through things taught in the previous class and help every student progress forward. Breakdown of each class is as follows.

  • Short briefing of class structure and revision from previous class.
  • Asana Practice with individual focussed attention.

The nature of yoga is to shine the light of awareness into the darkest corners of the body.

We'll also be covering scientific and practical aspects of each and every asana we practice during the classes. This will help every student to gain a much more sound understanding of the underlying principles behind the asanas.
